This scatterplot chart displays the top 5 games for 7 publishers and for each game it shows how well it did in terms of number of players it had and the rating it recieved. You can adjust the x-axis by clicking the up or down arrow keys which will appear when you hover over the textbox in the top left or you can enter a number instead. By hovering over a dot you can see the game that dot represents, which year it was published, the rating and number of players associated with it. Additionally, when you hover over a dot the associated group (publishing company) of which that dot is a part of will highlight.
